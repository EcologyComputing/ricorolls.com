About Rico Rolls

My name is Justin and I am the proud owner of Rico Rolls, a transportation business located in Cherokee County, GA. We specialize in providing reliable, safe, and cost-effective transportation solutions for both individuals and business owners.

With 6 professional drivers and a fleet of well-maintained vehicles, we serve North Metro Atlanta from Cumming to Acworth, Ball Ground, Waleska, and all points in between. We're expanding our services to include Alpharetta, Milton, and Roswell.
